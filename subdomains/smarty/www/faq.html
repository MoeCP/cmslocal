What is CopyPress?

CopyPress is a full-service content development company, creating content across a wide variety of industries for a bevy of clients. With a full staff of experienced content professionals, including keyword analysts, copywriters and editors as well as marketing, social media and technical professionals, we offer clients the most comprehensive content services on the market. In addition to content creation, we can also assist clients with content implementation, ensuring that their new content fuses with their website in the best way possible.

Who will see my assignments once they are submitted?

Once an article is submitted, your assigned editor will review it. Once the editing process is complete (72 hours) it will be sent to the client for their approval. Once the client approves the article they will publish it.

Yes. All submissions become the property of CopyPress.

Can I submit articles to CopyPress that I've submitted elsewhere?

No. CopyPress requires all writers to submit wholly original content that has not been published or distributed elsewhere.

Can I publish my articles elsewhere once I've submitted them to CopyPress?

No. Articles that you submit to CopyPress may not be republished or redistributed.

Typically, the editorial process will take about 3 business days. During this process, our editor will edit articles for spelling, grammar, accuracy, structure, flow, etc. At times this process may require the writer to fulfilling edit requests and other times where the articles will be approved as is or changed by the editor.

How often will I get paid?

CopyPress offers two monthly payments, on the 1st and 15th of each month. If the 1st or 15th falls on a weekend or Holiday CopyPress will schedule your payment for the first prior business day.

What forms of payment are offered?

CopyPress offers direct deposit and PayPal payments. If you wish to receive direct deposit you must complete a form and provide a copy of a voided check.

Does submitting an article mean I'll automatically be paid for that article?

How does CopyPress handle taxes?

You will be responsible for taxes on the income you receive from CopyPress. We require all writers to submit an electronic W-9 form before officially being hired. We will distribute 1099 forms for writers earning at least $600 in a calendar year. Further questions regarding your taxes should be discussed with your tax consultant.

Will I receive tax documents from  CopyPress at the end of the year?

Yes. If you have earned at least $600 in a calendar year we will send you the 1099 form to the name and address you have listed on your W9 form.

What HR forms are required to become a writer?

Contractor agreement, W9 Form, and Direct Deposit form.

Why does CopyPress require a W9 form?

CopyPress requires W9 forms to comply with the IRS standards. If we pay you at least $600 in a calendar year we will need to file a 1099 form with the IRS.

What does CopyPress do with the personal information provided on the W9 Form?

CopyPress stores your personal information electronically with confidential accounts. We will only share your personal information with the IRS in the event we will need to file a 1099 form for you.

Can I provide my business information on my W9 rather than my personal information?

Yes. You can submit your business information on your W9 form. However, the information you provide will provide the legal business information (name, address, etc) that corresponds to your business's EIN.
